BOOK APPOINTMENT AT OUR LONDON STORE

Our team of expert stylists are here to help you find your perfect outfit. The nature of our business means that we are in close contact with our brides for styling and for fitting gowns.

Masks must still be worn for all close contact that is one metre or less. We welcome our brides to bring up to two guests with them to their appointment. We charge a non-refundable fee of £30 for all first appointments. You can reschedule your appointment up to 72 hours before, but a cancellation will not be refunded. If you have any questions or need to amend your appointment, please contact us on london@sassiholford.com.
